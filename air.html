Air Pollution

Air pollution is a major threat to our world today, it is caused by many reasons but the most common causes for air pollution are , the burning of fossil fuels, Industrial emissions , transportation and more .

These carbon emissions from our transportation and factories cause global warming and eventually climate change.

A few actions we can take to help reduce air pollution are - Carpool/ reduce the number of trips with your car ,avoid burning trash and other materials , using energy efficient light bulbs etc.
