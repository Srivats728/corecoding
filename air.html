• Air Pollution

Air pollution is a major threat to our world today, it is caused by many different things but the most common causes for air pollution include ,the burning of fossil fuels, Industrial emissions,construction and demolition,transportation and more.

These carbon emissions from our transportation and factories cause global warming and eventually climate change.

A few actions we can take to help reduce air pollution are - Carpool/ reduce the number of trips with your car,avoid burning trash, wood ,leaves and other materials,using energy efficient light bulbs,reduce usage of plastic and more.
