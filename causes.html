Causes of pollution - Our world today has been highly influenced by

technology and human advancements , but these advancements

come with waste and unwanted products which causes pollution.

Pollution is a word which means the introduction/presence of a harmful

or poisonous substance in the environment. Pollutants can be natural

such as volcano ashes or sea salt particles,they can also be man-made

such as trash/waste products produced by factories and more.

There are 3 major types of pollution - Water Pollution, Air Pollution

and Land Pollution.
