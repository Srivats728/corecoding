There are 2 major topics we must focus on when it comes to effects of

pollution to our world,which are - Heath and the Environment .

The effects of pollution on our environment and our health are

countless as everything from a tiny litter to a oil leakage in the

ocean can damage/hurt our environment and economy, as well as our

health can get very damaged due to lack of clean water, lack of fresh air,

littering,etc.We must reduce the amount of products we waste as it harms

the environment and the lives of humans and animals alike.
