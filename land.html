• Land Pollution

Land pollution today is a major issue as it is caused by littering,

deforestation,soil erosion,overcrowded land fills,nuclear waste,

industrialization,urbanisation and more , which is also a major

issue for global warming and climate change. A few things we

can do to reduce land pollution are - practising reforestation and

afforestation,do not litter/if you see litter dispose of it properly,

reusing and recycling properly,proper soil waste treatment,

avoiding the usage of chemical fertilisers and pesticides and more.
